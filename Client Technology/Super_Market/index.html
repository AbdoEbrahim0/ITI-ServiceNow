Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery areas?

We currently deliver to most urban areas within a 50-mile radius of our main distribution center. Please check our delivery map for specific locations.


How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number on our website to track the status of your delivery in real-time.


What is your return policy?

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer service within 7 days of receiving your order. We accept returns for unopened and undamaged products.
